Knowing my Roots

Interveiw by Owen Griffin

What is your favorite food? Why?

My favorite food is corn beef and cabbage because I grew up eating it for holidays and my birthday and I hope to continue that.

What is your favorite music? Why do you like it?

My favorite music is 90s music because it is what I listen to every day in the car

What is your favorite vacation place? Why do you have any specific memories that you can share?

Florida because I remember when all of my grandchildren came down to visit me and George down in Florida it was a great time to connect with the family again.

What is one thing in the world that you would like to accomplish (when you are older?)

I would like to see all of my grandchildren graduate from college and see them get married after than that there is nothing else I would want to do

What is one thing in the world that makes you most happy?

Having my grandchildren and my own children visit me just because it brings the whole family home.
